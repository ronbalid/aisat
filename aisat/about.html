OBJECTIVES

Our objectives is to:

  1. Offer relevant programs, including practical training, using courses and content continually tailored to the present and future needs of society
  2. Prepare individuals with knowledge, skills attitudes and values in science and technology
  3. Educate individuals regardless of race, color ideology, and religion
  4. Maintain competent faculty that is committed to quality education and is responsive to the needs of the learners
  5. Provide students with adequate facilities that are conductive to teaching-learning process
  6. Evaluate periodically the school programs and policies to continuously promote an environment conductive to the growth of its stakeholders
  7. Contribute to the development and improvement of Philippine higher education
